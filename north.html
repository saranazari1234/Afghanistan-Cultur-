North Of Afghanistan

Northern Afghanistan is known for its scenic beauty, including fertile plains, hills, and cultural centers such as Mazar-i-Sharif. The people of the north are ethnically diverse, including Uzbeks, Tajiks, and Turkmen.

Navroz (Persian New Year) is widely celebrated here with music, food, and community events. Traditional clothing includes embroidered garments and vibrant patterns.

Important cities in the region include Mazar-i-Sharif, Sheberghan, and Kunduz.
