Afghanistans Culture

Afghanistan is a country with a rich and diverse culture shaped by its long history, geographic location, and ethnic diversity. From the snow-capped mountains in the north to the deserts in the south, every region offers unique traditions, clothes, music, and values. This website is a journey through Afghanistan’s four main cultural zones.
